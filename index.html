Harriton Research Lab

Harriton Research Lab was founded at Harriton High School in August of 2019 by Thaddaeus Kiker. Since then, the club has grown to six schools between PA, FL, and CA, where members are pursuing projects as varied as Genomics, Economics, and Astrophysics.
