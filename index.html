Past Projects

Young Stellar Objects

Between 2019 and 2021, a group of us from Harriton worked with Dr. Lynne Hillenbrand to investigate the variability of young stellar objects with the Zwicky Transient Facility. This project resulted in our club's first publication (in the Astronomical Journal)!
