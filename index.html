Harriton Research Lab

Harriton Research Laboratory is an interstate association for students to engage with science and coding together through interdisciplinary computational research experiences under the leadership of their peers and mentorship of professionals in academia. In doing so, we strive to synthesize and present our achievements in the appropriate academic mediums, such as scientific conferences and or refereed journals. What's Harriton, you might ask? Harriton Research Lab was founded at Harriton High School in 2019, and although we've kept our original name since, we have club chapters at multiple high schools across the United States.
